About

Maps first. Systems
ever since.

Hernane started out digitizing parcel maps for the Municipality of Tboli — validating tax declarations, processing land and machinery records, and keeping local government systems compliant, one barangay at a time. That grounding in geospatial data and public-sector process has carried through every role since.

A corporate campus in Davao brought 300-plus devices and a full network-admin workload. A provincial census office brought shapefile validation under deadline. A DENR field office brought its first public website. And a multi-brand food-service operation brought POS databases and a company-wide data-loss-prevention rollout. What ties it together isn't one technology — it's comfort moving between the physical (routers, laptops, CCTV runs) and the systemic (databases, web apps, compliance reporting), across government and private-sector environments that rarely share a playbook.
